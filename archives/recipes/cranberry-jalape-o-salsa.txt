Cranberry Jalapeño Salsa

This salsa is great on chips, but also makes a great sauce for a protein (think pork or chicken).

Source: Food Network
URL: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/articles/50-things-to-make-with-cranberries.html

Yield: 16oz

INGREDIENTS

2 C fresh cranberries
1/4 C sugar
1/3 C finely chopped cucumber
1/3 C finely chopped cilantro
1/4 C finely copped onion
1 finely minced jalapeño
1 Tbsp lime juice (fresh)
1/2 tsp kosher salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine cranberries and sugar in a food processor and finely chop (if necessary add a splash of water).
2. Combine with the rest of the ingredients.

NOTES

Adjust the heat of the chili to taste. Less is more with the onion.
