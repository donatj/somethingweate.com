Red Cabbage

Source: Grandma Cherekos

INGREDIENTS

2 Lbs Shredded Red Cabbage
4-6 (or more) strips of bacon
4 Tbsp soy sauce
3 Tbsp brown sugar
3 Tbsp white sugar
3 Tbsp vinegar
1 small onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

1. Fry the bacon, reserving the grease
2. Add cabbage and sprinkle with salt; cook over high heat. Stir occasionally.
3. Stir in the rest of the ingredients; cover and cook until tender.

NOTES

Grandma Cherekos uses "a bit" of liquid smoke.
