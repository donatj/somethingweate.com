DIY Pace Picante Sauce

INGREDIENTS

1 large (1#12) can of crushed tomatoes
1 regular can of Rotelle tomatoes
1 small can of diced green chillies
1 fresh jalapeno, diced
1 med-large clove garlic, crushed or smashed
1/3 large yellow onion, diced
2 tsp kosher salt

DIRECTIONS

1. Combine, mix, eat

NOTES

2 tsp salt wan't quite salty enough but 3 was WAY too much. Play with it to taste. For extra heat either leave the heat in the jalapeno or add a full habanero.
